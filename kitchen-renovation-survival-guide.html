The challenge: living without a kitchen

Renovations are exciting — until your kitchen disappears. With 6–10 weeks of building work, families are left without cooking facilities, sinks, or safe prep space. The result? Expensive takeaways, disorganised routines, and a lot of washing up in buckets or the bath. It doesn't have to be this way.

The easiest solution: a temporary kitchen pod on your driveway

Keep life normal while the builders work. A Kitchen Rescue pod gives you full cooking functionality, proper washing up, and room to stay organised — without dust and disruption inside your home.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a kitchen renovation usually take?

Most UK kitchen renovations take 6–10 weeks depending on scope, trades, and lead times. A temporary kitchen helps you keep cooking throughout.

What utilities do I need for a temporary kitchen pod?

Two standard 13A double sockets within 25m, an outdoor tap for water connection, and a drain within 3m.

Is a temporary kitchen pod cheaper than takeaways?

For a family eating daily takeaways, weekly spend can easily exceed a pod hire. A pod lets you cook normal meals and often saves money overall.

How quickly can the pod be installed?

Typically installed in a few hours once access is confirmed and paperwork is signed.

Can you help with insurance or flood/fire claims?

Yes. We can liaise with your loss adjuster and provide the documents they need for authorisation.
