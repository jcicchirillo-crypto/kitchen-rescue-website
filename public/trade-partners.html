How it works with your projects

Simple, plug-and-play. You don't have to touch any connections. We handle installation, setup and collection.

Step 1
You share the option

When you quote, mention that a temporary kitchen pod is available. You can show our info sheet or send your unique booking link.

Step 2
Client books directly

Your client contacts us to check dates and pricing. We confirm everything with them and arrange delivery to the driveway.

Step 3
We install & connect

We set up the pod, plug into a standard power supply and run waste to a drain within 9 metres. If it's further away, we can use a pump.

Step 4
You get the referral

Once your client's booking is confirmed with your code, we log the referral and you receive £50 for every completed hire.
