Who We Work With

Kitchen Rescue supports loss adjusters, insurance repair contractors, insurers, brokers, councils and housing associations. If your client is without a kitchen, we can help.

How It Works

  • Step 1 – Assessment: You provide the address and expected repair duration.
  • Step 2 – Delivery: We deliver and install the pod on the driveway.
  • Step 3 – Use: The policyholder lives normally with cooking, washing & cleaning facilities.
  • Step 4 – Collection: We remove the pod once reinstatement is complete.
