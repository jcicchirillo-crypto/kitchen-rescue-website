Induction Hob

Bosch PUG61RAA5B Series 2 Four Zone with Boost Zone

▼

59cm four zone induction hob with a boost zone for rapid heating. Touch controls, plug and play. You'll need induction-compatible cookware — test with a magnet on the base of your pans, it should stick.

Fridge Freezer

Fridgemaster MC55265EB — 269L 70/30

▼

269 litre total capacity with a 70/30 fridge-freezer split. Keep the fridge between 3–5°C and the freezer at -18°C for best results. Give it 4 hours to reach temperature before loading with food.
