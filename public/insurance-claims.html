The Problem

When a kitchen is removed, daily life stops

During kitchen reinstatement works, policyholders face weeks without basic facilities — creating a ripple effect that costs insurers time, money, and customer satisfaction.

🏠

No basic facilities

Weeks without an oven, sink, washing machine, or dishwasher — affecting the whole family.

😤

Increased complaints

Frustration leads to higher complaint rates and customer dissatisfaction scores.

🐾

Accommodation issues

Hotels often won't accept pets, forcing difficult choices for families.

💸

Higher claim spend

Alternative accommodation and extended claims drive up overall costs significantly.
