When a kitchen is removed, daily life stops

During insured kitchen reinstatement works, policyholders can face weeks without basic facilities — leading to increased complaints and distress, pressure to provide alternative accommodation, extended claim duration, and poorer outcomes for families and vulnerable households. A temporary kitchen removes this pressure point.

Frequently Asked Questions

We guarantee delivery and installation within 48 hours of claim notification. For urgent cases, we can often deliver same-day or next-day in many locations across the UK.

Our units are 3m x 2m weatherproof units, fully self-contained and insulated.

Rental periods are completely flexible based on the claim duration. Most rentals are between 4-12 weeks, but we can accommodate shorter or longer periods as needed.

We provide coverage across England and Wales. Our regional distribution centers ensure fast delivery times throughout these areas.

Pricing varies based on rental duration and specific requirements. However, our solution is typically 40-60% less expensive than providing alternative accommodation, while delivering better customer outcomes.

We work closely with insurance teams to ensure vulnerable customers receive appropriate solutions. While our units are not currently wheelchair accessible, please let us know your requirements and we can find a suitable solution.

Simply contact our team via phone or the contact form. We'll discuss your requirements, set up an account, and provide training for your claims handlers on our notification process.
