When a kitchen is removed, daily life stops

During insured kitchen reinstatement works, policyholders can face weeks without basic facilities — leading to increased complaints and distress, pressure to provide alternative accommodation, extended claim duration, and poorer outcomes for families and vulnerable households. A temporary kitchen removes this pressure point.

What Kitchen Rescue provides

Kitchen Rescue supplies fully self-contained, plug-in temporary kitchen pods for insurance claims. Each pod provides a fully working kitchen setup so households can continue normal daily living while reinstatement works are completed.

What's inside each pod

  • • Oven and hob
  • • Sink with hot and cold water
  • • Fridge-freezer
  • • Dishwasher
  • • Washing machine
  • • Worktop and storage

Why Kitchen Rescue is used on claims

  • • Enables policyholders to remain in their home
  • • Reduces reliance on alternative accommodation
  • • Helps control overall claim costs
  • • Reduces complaints and distress
  • • Particularly suitable for families, elderly, or vulnerable customers
  • • Simple to deploy alongside reinstatement programmes

How it works on a claim

  1. 1

    Kitchen reinstatement works are approved

  2. 2

    Kitchen Rescue supplies a temporary kitchen pod

  3. 3

    Pod is installed prior to kitchen rip-out

  4. 4

    Policyholder remains in-situ during works

  5. 5

    Pod is removed once the permanent kitchen is reinstated

No ongoing management required from the insurer.

Ownership & control

  • • Kitchen Rescue owns and manages its own pods (no subcontracting)
  • • Availability, delivery, and maintenance are controlled by us
  • • Designed specifically for use during reinstatement works

Frequently asked questions

Is this suitable instead of alternative accommodation? +

In many kitchen-only claims, a temporary kitchen allows the policyholder to remain safely in their home, avoiding the need to decant.

What services are required? +

A standard electrical supply and a water connection. We advise at point of enquiry.

Who arranges delivery and collection? +

Kitchen Rescue manages delivery, installation, and collection directly.

Is this suitable for vulnerable policyholders? +

Yes — this is one of the primary use cases, particularly where disruption would cause distress or hardship.
