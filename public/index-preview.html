Specs & requirements

Simple setup. We handle all the connections.

  • Size: 3.6m x 1.8m x 2.2H (12' x 6' x 6'6")
  • Power: Two double sockets (inside or out – we can sort extension leads)
  • Water: An outside tap within 25 metres
  • Drainage: A normal drain within 9 metres (we can pump if further)
  • Install time: Typically 2–3 hours
  • Access: Clear, level driveway on private land
  • Surface: Tarmac, concrete, or firm gravel (gentle slope is fine)
