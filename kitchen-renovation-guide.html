Kitchen Renovation Guide 2024: Complete Planning & Temporary Solutions

Planning a kitchen renovation? This comprehensive guide covers everything from timeline to temporary kitchen solutions to keep your family fed and functioning.

Kitchen Renovation Timeline

Typical Kitchen Renovation Phases:

  • Week 1-2: Demolition and structural work
  • Week 3-4: Plumbing, electrical, and flooring
  • Week 5-6: Kitchen unit installation
  • Week 7-8: Worktop fitting and appliance installation
  • Week 9: Final touches and snagging

Average Duration: 6-10 weeks for a complete renovation
